Nutrition

Overview

Healthy eating and balanced diet are especially important for children to receive the nutrients they need for growth and development. Children who think about nutrition will be more likely to practice good eating habits in the future.

Tips

  1. Drink water and stay hydrated. Stay away from sugary drinks such as soda and artificial juices.
  2. Build a colorful plate with lots of fruits and vegetables.They should make up half of your plate.
  3. Limit how much your child snacks. Teach them smart portion sizes and replace junk food in the house with healthier options.
  4. Be a role mode. Prepare nutritious foods at home and eat healthy yourself.
